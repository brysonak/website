Hello, if you've been following this project, you might wonder why there hasn't been anything new to Azin for a whole week since the Lexer. You might also wonder:

## Where's the Parser?
We haven't stop working on Azin, of course not. But we have decided, to first settle on syntax before making the parser, and this takes time. I can't predict how long it will take but it won't be long. Discussion takes place on our discord server. If you want to suggest ideas, ask questions, or be up to date with decisions, join our discord server on the [links](https://azin-lang.org/links) tab. If you noticed, the website is getting more updates than the compiler. This is why. I am just trying to kill time.

For the most stable and up-to-date language design, keep an eye on the [syntax documentation](https://docs.azin-lang.org/language/syntax/overview), which is updated as decisions become finalized (but can still be **changed**)
